## Full Description (Amazon Appstore — 1500–4000 characters)

**Temple TV — Anywhere You Go, Stay Connected to Faith**

Experience the power of worship and the Word of God at your fingertips. Temple TV is the official streaming app of Jesus Christ Temple Ministry (JCTM) — bringing you live services, on-demand sermons, worship music, and inspirational teachings from anointed ministers, wherever life takes you.

**🔴 WATCH LIVE, ANYTIME**
Never miss a service again. Temple TV streams live worship events, Sunday services, special conferences, and revival nights in real time. A single tap puts you in the front row — wherever you are.

**📚 ON-DEMAND SERMON LIBRARY**
Explore hundreds of faith-building messages on topics that matter: breakthrough, family, purpose, prayer, deliverance, and more. Rewatch your favourites or share a message that changed your life.

**🙏 WORSHIP THAT MOVES YOU**
Our worship sessions and music videos carry the authentic presence of God. Plug in your earphones, close your eyes, and let the music minister to your spirit.

**📺 BUILT FOR EVERY SCREEN**
Temple TV is optimised for phone, tablet, Amazon Fire TV, Android TV, and Smart TV screens. Start watching on your phone and continue on the big screen — the experience is seamless.

**🔔 NEVER MISS A BROADCAST**
Enable notifications and we'll alert you the moment a live service begins. Be the first to know about special events, guest ministers, and late-night prayer sessions.

**FEATURES AT A GLANCE**
• Live streaming of services and special events
• Full sermon archive — search by series, topic, or speaker
• Worship and praise music videos
• TV Guide — know what's broadcasting next
• Search across the entire content library
• Clean, distraction-free viewing experience
• Fast loading even on slower connections
• Free to use — no subscription required

**ABOUT JESUS CHRIST TEMPLE MINISTRY**
JCTM is a Spirit-filled, Bible-believing ministry dedicated to preaching the undiluted Gospel of Jesus Christ and equipping believers for kingdom impact. Through Temple TV, we extend the walls of the church to reach souls across Nigeria and the world.

*"Faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God." — Romans 10:17*

Download Temple TV today and let every day begin and end in the presence of God.

## Build Commands

```bash
# Install EAS CLI (one-time)
npm install -g eas-cli

# Log in to Expo account
eas login

# Build Fire TV APK (release)
cd artifacts/mobile
eas build --platform android --profile firetv

# The signed APK downloads to your machine after the cloud build.
# Upload it to Amazon Developer Console → New Submission → APK.
```
