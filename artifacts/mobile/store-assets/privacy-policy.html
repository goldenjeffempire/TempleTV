Privacy Policy — Jesus Christ Temple Ministry

Effective Date: April 22, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 22, 2026  |  Version: 1.0

Welcome to Temple TV, a faith-based streaming platform operated by Jesus Christ Temple Ministry (JCTM). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By downloading, installing, or using the Temple TV application ("App"), you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account registration details (name, email address) when you create an account
  • Profile information you choose to add (optional display name, profile photo)
  • Messages or feedback you send to us via the contact form or support email

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Anonymous usage data (screens visited, features used) to improve the App
  • Crash reports and error logs to diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Network connection type (Wi-Fi/mobile) to optimize video streaming quality

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, microphone audio, camera images, or financial information. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Temple TV streaming service
  • Authenticate your account and enable personalized features (watchlist, history)
  • Send you push notifications about live broadcasts or new content — only if you opt in
  • Respond to your support requests and improve customer service
  • Analyse anonymous, aggregated usage trends to improve the App experience
  • Diagnose and resolve technical problems

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3 Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, push notification delivery) who process data solely to operate the App on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of JCTM or others.
  • Organizational Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data would be transferred under the same privacy protections.

No other sharing occurs without your explicit consent.

4 Third-Party Video Content

Temple TV streams content from YouTube and other authorised video platforms. When you watch embedded videos, those platforms may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We use privacy-enhanced embed modes wherever available (e.g. youtube-nocookie.com) to minimise data collection.

We encourage you to review YouTube's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy .

5 Push Notifications

Temple TV may send push notifications to alert you about live broadcasts, new sermons, or important ministry announcements. You can enable or disable these at any time in your device's notification settings or within the App.

We will never use push notifications for advertising or send them more frequently than is reasonably necessary to keep you informed.

6 Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Anonymous usage analytics are retained in aggregated, non-identifiable form for up to 24 months to help us track long-term product improvements.

7 Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encrypted HTTPS transmission, server-side hashed passwords, and access controls limiting who on our team can view user data.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you have reason to believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please notify us immediately at the contact address below.

8 Children's Privacy

Temple TV is a general-audience application intended for users of all ages within a family-friendly faith context. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at templedeveloper@jctm.org.ng and we will respond within 30 days.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notice or push notification at least 7 days before they take effect.

Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
