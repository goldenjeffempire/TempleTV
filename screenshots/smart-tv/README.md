# Smart TV Screenshots

Place TV screenshots here for future Tizen (Samsung) and webOS (LG) store listings.
The production Smart TV target is the web app at https://tv.templetv.org.ng.

## Specifications

| Platform | Dimensions | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Samsung Galaxy Store (Tizen) | 1920 × 1080 px | PNG |
| LG Content Store (webOS) | 1920 × 1080 px | PNG |
| Amazon Fire TV | 1920 × 1080 px | JPG/PNG |
| Android TV (Play Console) | 1920 × 1080 px | JPG/PNG |
