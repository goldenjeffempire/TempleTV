# 🎬 Broadcast Queue System Enhancement

## Objective

Redesign the broadcast queue behavior so uploaded videos follow a professional television scheduling workflow instead of immediately appearing in active broadcast playback.

---

## 📥 Queue Management Logic

Implement a true broadcast queue system where:

* Newly uploaded videos are automatically added to the broadcast queue
* Uploaded content must wait for its scheduled playback turn
* Videos should not interrupt the currently broadcasting content immediately after upload

---

## 🔄 Queue Playback Rules

* Ensure videos play strictly according to queue order
* Maintain continuous non-stop broadcasting flow
* Automatically advance to the next queued content only after the current broadcast finishes

---

## 🚫 Prevent Immediate Broadcast Injection

* Do not instantly push newly uploaded videos into active ON AIR playback
* Prevent uploads from disrupting currently scheduled or active broadcast content

---

## 📺 Television Station Scheduling Behavior

The queue system must behave like a real television station programming schedule:

* Content waits in lineup order
* Playback transitions occur only when appropriate
* Broadcasting remains stable and predictable

---

## ⚙️ Queue System Reliability

Ensure the queue system supports:

* Stable scheduling operations
* Real-time queue synchronization across all devices
* Queue persistence during refreshes or reconnects
* Continuous playback even during upload activity

## 🌍 Cross-Platform Consistency

Ensure queue behavior remains identical across:

* Web platform
