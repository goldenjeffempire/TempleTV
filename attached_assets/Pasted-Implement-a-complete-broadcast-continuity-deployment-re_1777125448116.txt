# 📺 Broadcast Queue Stability Enhancement

## Objective

Ensure newly uploaded videos never interrupt or replace the currently broadcasting content and instead enter a structured broadcast queue system that behaves like a real-world television station programming schedule.

---

## Queue Integrity Requirements

* Ensure that when a new video is uploaded:

  * The currently broadcasting video continues playing uninterrupted
  * The active ON AIR stream is never replaced immediately by the newly uploaded content
  * Newly uploaded videos are automatically added to the end of the broadcast queue

* Maintain strict queue order execution where:

  * Each video waits for its scheduled playback turn
  * Broadcast progression remains continuous and stable

---

## Continuous Broadcast Protection

* Prevent uploads from triggering:

  * Forced player reloads
  * Queue resets
  * Broadcast interruptions
  * Stream restarts
  * Playback switching during active broadcast

* Ensure the ON AIR stream remains stable regardless of upload activity.

---
